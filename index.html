Our Team

The people behind
every project.

Three decades of aluminium architecture, shaped by a team that takes each project personally.

Mr. Geet Kumar Pabra

Founder · Since 1989

Mr. Geet Kumar Pabra founded M/s Indrajit Associates in 1989 in Bhopal, with a singular vision — to bring precision-engineered aluminium architectural solutions to a market that was still in its early stages in India.

Starting with a modest fabrication setup in Govindpura Industrial Area, he built the company project by project, earning trust through consistent quality and on-time delivery. What began as a modest operation steadily grew into a pan-India enterprise.

Mr. Geetansh Pabra

Director · Since 2020

Mr. Geetansh Pabra joined Indrajit Alusol in 2020, bringing fresh perspective to a company with three decades of legacy. Since joining, he has been the key driving force behind the diversification and modernisation of the business.

Under his aegis, the company has expanded its capabilities — strengthening client relationships, streamlining project execution, and pushing into new markets and project typologies. His focus on operational efficiency, quality control, and business development has spearheaded Indrajit Alusol take on increasingly ambitious projects across India.
